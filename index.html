1968
Intel Founded
Intel is founded in Mountain View, California. From the beginning, Intel focused on innovation and responsible manufacturing practices.
1994
First Environmental Report
Intel publishes its first environmental, health, and safety report, demonstrating a commitment to transparency and sustainability.
2003
Green Power Leadership Award
Intel receives the EPA Green Power Leadership Award for its early adoption of renewable energy in its operations.
2008
Largest Voluntary Purchaser of Green Power
Intel becomes the largest voluntary purchaser of green power in the U.S., supporting the growth of renewable energy markets.
2012
Water Conservation Milestone
Intel achieves a milestone in water conservation, saving billions of gallons of water through efficiency projects.
2022
100% Renewable Electricity
Intel achieved 100% renewable electricity use in its global operations, reducing its carbon footprint and supporting clean energy initiatives.
2030
Net Positive Water Use
Intel aims to restore more freshwater than it consumes, supporting water conservation projects in local communities.
2030
Zero Waste to Landfill
Intel is working to send zero waste from its global manufacturing operations to landfill by 2030.
2040
Net-Zero Greenhouse Gas Emissions
Intel’s goal is to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across its global operations by 2040.
